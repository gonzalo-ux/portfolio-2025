Unified documentation

An internal product that streamlined and unified documentation for systems and brand teams

Along with my engineer partner we built a bespoke solution that allowed teams to create, manage and deploy documentation to separate websites through a CMS while embedding external sources giving them freedom to own and reuse content across sites.

Check the public Brand Zalando website

The challenge

We had scattered and disconnected documentation

Since always the Zalando design system team add detailed documentation about, components, styles, patterns, etc. However this documentation was scattered and separated by platform, a website for web, markdown files for apps and figma for design. This made really hard to find the right answer for consumers and a big headache for system creators to maintain these spaces of documentation. This was shared pain among other teams like the Zalando brand and the content teams.

My role, achievements and outcomes

I partnered with a front end engineer to built the whole product from the ground up. I designed all the screens and modules as content types (templates), including visual design, information architecture and how the rules about how to manage the content. I trained around 40 people about how to use our solutions and the CMS and help to built Figma tooling to embed images, slides and prototypes on the website from Figma and Google docs. I played the role of Design lead, Project manager, Content designer and Front-end engineer.
