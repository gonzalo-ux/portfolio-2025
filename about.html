Gonzalo Vasquez

Creative design systems technologist, mentor and speaker.

Linkedin • Resume • hello@gonzalo.com

I'm a Chilean/Irish designer in Dublin, specializing in designing and building software at scale. As part of the Zalando Design System team, I architect our theming and token infrastructure, enhance documentation, and push the boundaries of our Figma libraries. All this while enabling feature teams to build amazing user experiences through support or being embedded in their projects.

Driven by systems thinking, my goal is to connect technology and business through common tools and processes. I thrive on collaboration and believe that communication is the key to overcoming any technical problem—because design is ultimately about people.

With a 7-year focus on Design Systems, my career began in Graphic Design and multimedia, spanning flash, motion graphics, and 3D. I transitioned into UX Design through e-learning and SaaS products, giving me a deep understanding of the full design lifecycle.

When I’m out of work I enjoy good food, cooking, travelling and attending live music gigs. But overall I love spend time with my wife and our daughter.
